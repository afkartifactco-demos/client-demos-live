Privacy outline

A clear plan for data, advertising and reader choice.

This pre-launch policy explains the intended data practices for the publication. It is deliberately conservative: collect only what supports the reader experience, protect it, and make advertising disclosures easy to understand.

Pre-launch noteThis is a professional pre-launch outline, not legal advice. The owner should approve it and obtain legal review before collecting reader data or activating advertising.

Information readers provide

If newsletter or contact tools are activated, the site may receive an email address, home area and information included in a message or news tip.

Local demo storage

The publisher demonstration can save sample drafts and preferences only in the current browser. Those demo records are not uploaded to a server.

Analytics and advertising

If analytics or Google AdSense is activated, those services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure visits, prevent fraud and personalize or measure advertising. Consent and opt-out controls should be configured before launch.

Weather and external links

Live forecast information may come from the National Weather Service. Links to government, social, mapping and sponsor websites follow those organizations' privacy practices.

Retention and security

Reader information should be retained only as long as needed for the requested service, legal obligations and basic security. No system can promise absolute security.

Reader choices

  • Unsubscribe from email alerts at any time
  • Decline non-essential cookies when consent controls are active
  • Request access, correction or deletion where applicable
  • Contact the publication with privacy questions
